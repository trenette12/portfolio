Where did it all start?

2004

The Era of MySpace
Admittedly during this year, I became fascinated with the option to add multimedia to my then MySpace page. I thought it was kind of magical that inserting one piece of code could translate into an element that was customized by me. The manipulation of content, images, videos, audio was intriguing to me so much so that I mentioned my interest to my best friend and she asked me "Why don't you study for that in college"? This conversation along with other members of my family inspired me to begin a journey of studying and experimenting with Web Development.

2014

Class of 2014!
During various trials and road bumps along the way, I finally received my Bachelor of Science degree in Digital Media and Web Technology. Later that year, I was granted an internship at a non-profit organization as a Web Content Specialist and have been keeping up to date with web technologies and growing in this field ever since. There's no looking back!

2017

Who wants Full Stack? Of what Pancakes?
A full stack in this case is not in relation to pancakes. This is in reference to Full Stack Web Development. In this year, I took another leap and wanted to challenge myself as a full stack web developer. I was used to working on the front-end but had no real knowledge or experience of what the back-end was, how it worked and most importantly how it communicated with the front-end to create the ultimate user experience. I enrolled in a six month boot camp to further my knowledge and no longer limit myself to just piece of web development. It's a bit mind blowing and exciting how the technologies talk to one another. All questions are never answered, and one bit of advice, there's always more than one answer or one way to perform an operation. There's more to come on this journey but this brings you up to date.
